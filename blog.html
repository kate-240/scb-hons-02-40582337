    Some thing's you should know

    When it comes to concerts here is what you should do:

      Travel with a friend or family member

      Plan the journey ahead of time

      keep important items hidden

    Travel with a friend or family member

    Travelling with a friend or family member is the best thing to do if you going with them to the concert

    It is better to go with someone to a concert eith being a family member or a friend if you have multiple tickets to that event, or if you are going alone message someone you trust with your location and where the venue is.

    Plan the journey ahead of time

    Planning ahead of time helps you keep track of everything
    Image

    Planning ahead of time when it comes to events is important since it helps you to know the times of when to leave and if using public transport the times when they leave as well or if there will be any delays with transport

    Keep important items hidden

    The importance of keeping important personal items hidden from view at concerts.
    Image

    Keeping important items hidden will help in the long run when you are travellingwith someone or travelling alone,keeping these items in small bags or pockets will keep them out of sight of people you don't trust.

    Some venues allow bag in the size of a sheet of A4 paper, the small the better so it is easier of the security can look through it easily and quickly.

    Tips

    Buy a RFID wallet or purse if you are going to us a credit or debit cards

    Always bring a bag to keep you items in even if you don't feel like you need it

    Bags should be the size of a A4 sheet of paper or smaller

    Add tickets to your Apple Wallet/ Google Wallet beforehand so it is easier to get in

    Eat before going to the concert

    Share location with friends and family
